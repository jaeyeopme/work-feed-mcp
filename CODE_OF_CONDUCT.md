# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We pledge to make participation in this project welcoming, respectful, and harassment-free for everyone.

## Expected behavior

Participants are expected to:

- use welcoming and inclusive language;
- respect different viewpoints and experience levels;
- give and accept constructive feedback;
- focus disagreements on project outcomes and evidence;
- avoid sharing private information without permission.

## Unacceptable behavior

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, abusive comments, discriminatory language, intimidation, sustained disruption, or publishing another person's private information without consent.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, reject contributions, or restrict participation when behavior violates this code. Reports should use the repository maintainer channels available on GitHub. Security-sensitive reports should follow `SECURITY.md`.
